Schemes.
AICTE brings you various schemes and special scholarship to promote technical education.

General Schemes

KAUSHAL AUGMENTATION AND RESTRUCTURING MISSION OF AICTE (KARMA)

LAICTE is initiating “Kaushal Augmentation and Restructuring Mission of AICTE” (KARMA) for all AICTE approved institutions in the country to overcome the dual challenge of scarcity of skilled manpower in jobs and low skill level of those who are presently in jobs.


PRADHAN MANTRI KAUSHAL VIKAS YOJNA (PMKVY)

AICTE is implementing the PMKVY for Technical Institutes scheme through AICTE approved Technical Institutions in the country.Under this Scheme in next three years, 10.5 Lakh youth are to be given Engineering Skills.
